# 🤝 Contributing to Our Project

First of all, thank you for considering contributing! 🎉
We welcome contributions from developers of all skill levels. To maintain a healthy, respectful, and productive community, please follow the guidelines below.

---

## 🌍 Community Standards

We are committed to creating a **welcoming, inclusive, and respectful** environment for everyone.

By participating in this project, you agree to:

### ✅ Be Respectful

* Treat everyone with kindness and professionalism.
* Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
* Accept constructive feedback gracefully.

### ✅ Be Inclusive

* Use welcoming and inclusive language.
* Avoid discriminatory or exclusionary behavior.
* Support contributors from diverse backgrounds.

### ✅ Be Collaborative

* Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
* Provide helpful feedback in code reviews.
* Encourage new contributors.

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
* Personal attacks or insulting comments
* Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
* Publishing private information without permission
* Any other conduct deemed inappropriate by project maintainers

---

## 📢 Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

* Opening a private issue (if enabled), or
* Contacting the maintainers directly via email

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.

---

## 🛠 Code of Conduct

This project follows a Code of Conduct inspired by the Contributor Covenant.
Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with these standards.
